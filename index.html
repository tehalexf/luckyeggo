LuckyEggo is a evolution EXP calculator. It will tell you what you must do to get the maximum experience from your inventory! Simply follow the steps below to get started. Best of all, it's open source!
NOTE: If a pokemon cannot be evolved, (i.e., Beedrill), it will not show up in the auto-fill.

  • Pokemon: The name of a pokemon in your inventory.
  • # in Inventory: How many of this type of pokemon are in your inventory?.
  • Candies owned: How many candies for this pokemon do you have?
  • Have Evolved: Do you have the evolved form of this pokemon?
  • Recycle Evolved: Do you want to transfer the evolved form for more candy?
  • Add another: Ad another row to add more entries.
  • Generate Instructions: When you're done filling this stuff out, you can generate a list of steps!
